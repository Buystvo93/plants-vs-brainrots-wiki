Overview

The Grape is an obtainable Mythical plant that can be bought from the Seed Shop for $2500000 or 249 Robux.

Appearance

The Grape starts with a base of green foliage underneath similar to the Tomatrio, it then has a thick spiraled stem containing two green leaves. There are two grapes hanging on each of the leaves, one purple grape that appears to be angry while the other blue grape seems to be smiling

Obtainment

3.5% chance of appearing in the Seed Shop.
