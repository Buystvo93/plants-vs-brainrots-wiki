OVERVIEW

The Cactus is an obtainable Rare plant that can be bought from the Seed Shop for $200 or 5 Robux.

APPEARANCE

The base of the Cactus is a spherical dark green ball with several white spikes protruding out of its surface, it has 2 eyes with huge pupils and highlights above it is a bright pink flower. Behind the Cactus is a stem holding a smaller cactus base appearing as a catapult which is used for shooting at Brainrots. The seed is a small rectangular cuboid, which is 2 studs, and has a lime green to a light greenish-yellow gradient.

OBTAINEMENT

100% Chance of being in the seed shop.
