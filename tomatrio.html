Overview

The Tomatrio is an obtainable Secret plant that can be bought from the Seed Shop for $125000000 or 749 Robux.

Appearance

The Tomatrio has a long green stem along with 9 leaves hanging on it, there is an area of foliage at the bottom creating a blooming flower shape. At the middle is 3 tomatoes that have facial features appearing to be angry, the rightmost tomato is wearing a green top hat.

Obtainment

0.75% chance of appearing in the Seed Shop.
